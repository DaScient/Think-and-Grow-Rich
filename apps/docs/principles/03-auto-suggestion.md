# 3. Auto-Suggestion — The Medium for Influencing the Subconscious Mind

> *"Your subconscious mind recognizes and acts only upon thoughts which have been well mixed with emotion or feeling."* — Napoleon Hill

## What It Is

Auto-suggestion is the agency of communication between the conscious and subconscious mind. It is the medium through which you can deliberately program your subconscious with the thoughts you want it to act upon.

The subconscious does not understand mere words — it responds to **feelings**. Words mixed with intense emotion bypass the analytical conscious mind and are accepted directly by the subconscious as instructions.

## Key Insight

The subconscious responds to feelings, not words alone. Combine vivid visualization with deep emotion for maximum subconscious programming.

## The Four Steps

::: tip Action Steps
1. **Read your written desire statement** aloud in a quiet, concentrated state — morning and night.
2. **See yourself already in possession** of the money or goal — create a vivid mental picture.
3. **Feel the emotions** of already having it — gratitude, joy, certainty, relief.
4. **Repeat this ritual** morning and night without exception, until it becomes automatic.
:::

## Why Emotion is the Key

::: info The Mechanism
When you repeat your desire with genuine emotional intensity:
1. The vibration of your thought is raised
2. The subconscious registers it as a command
3. Infinite Intelligence is called into action
4. The subconscious begins arranging circumstances in your favor
:::

## Daily Practice Template

```
Morning (upon waking):
1. Read desire statement aloud — twice
2. Close eyes, visualize the goal as already achieved — 2 minutes
3. Feel the emotions of its attainment — gratitude, certainty

Evening (before sleep):
1. Repeat the morning process
2. The subconscious is most receptive just before sleep
```
