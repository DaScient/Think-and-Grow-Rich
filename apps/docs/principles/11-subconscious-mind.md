# 11. The Subconscious Mind — The Connecting Link

> *"The subconscious mind works day and night. Through a method of procedure unknown to man, the subconscious mind draws upon the forces of Infinite Intelligence for the power with which it voluntarily transmutes one's desires into their physical equivalent."* — Napoleon Hill

## What It Is

The subconscious mind is the connecting link between the finite mind of man and Infinite Intelligence. It functions day and night, continuously transmuting your dominant thoughts into their physical equivalents.

It does not distinguish between constructive and destructive thoughts. It works with whatever material you provide. Feed it fear — it works to realize fear. Feed it desire and faith — it works to realize desire and faith.

## Key Insight

The subconscious does not distinguish between thoughts of fear and thoughts of faith. You choose which to feed it.

## The Three Steps

::: tip Action Steps
1. **Feed the subconscious daily** through auto-suggestion, affirmation, and visualization — especially at bedtime.
2. **Saturate your thoughts** with the seven positive emotions: desire, faith, love, sex, enthusiasm, romance, hope.
3. **Actively block the seven negative emotions**: fear, jealousy, hatred, revenge, greed, superstition, anger.
:::

## The Seven Positive Emotions

| Emotion | Role |
|---------|------|
| Desire | The ignition |
| Faith | The amplifier |
| Love | The sustainer |
| Sex | The creative charge |
| Enthusiasm | The accelerant |
| Romance | The beautifier |
| Hope | The bridge to the future |

::: warning The Seven Negative Emotions
Fear, jealousy, hatred, revenge, greed, superstition, anger — these must be identified and immediately replaced with their positive counterparts. They do not fade on their own; they must be actively displaced.
:::

## Daily Affirmation

*"My subconscious mind works day and night to transform my desires into reality. I feed it only thoughts of power, love, and abundance."*
